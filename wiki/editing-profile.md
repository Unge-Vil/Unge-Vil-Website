# Editing Your Profile

After logging in you can update personal details and preferences.

1. Click your name in the top-right corner and choose **Profile**.
2. Update your display name, short biography, and contact information.
3. Upload a profile picture using the **Profile Image** section so your avatar shows on team grids.
4. If you see a **Location** dropdown, pick your department; this controls where your card appears.
5. Toggle **Primary Contact** (when available) if you are the main person for that department.
6. Press **Update Profile** at the bottom of the page to save your changes.
7. Use **Change Password** if you want to update your credentials.

Changes take effect immediately across the site and are translated automatically for English readers.
